Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 5 March 2026

Welcome to mBrain Quiz. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the mBrain Quiz mobile application collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

mBrain Quiz does not require users to provide personal information such as name, phone number, or address to play the game.

However, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically for app functionality, analytics, and advertising purposes.

  • Device information
  • Operating system version
  • IP address
  • Advertising ID
  • App interaction data

2. Coins and Reward System

mBrain Quiz allows users to earn virtual coins by completing in-app activities such as playing quizzes or completing tasks available within the app.

Coins earned inside the app may be used to redeem digital rewards such as gift cards or vouchers when available.

The developer reserves the right to modify reward rules, coin values, or redemption requirements at any time to maintain fair usage of the system.

3. Offerwall and Third-Party Tasks

The app may provide access to third-party offerwalls that allow users to complete tasks in exchange for coins.

These offers are provided by external partners through PubScale. Third-party providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

mBrain Quiz is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party services.

4. Advertising

The app displays advertisements using third-party advertising platforms.

  • Google Ad Manager
  • PubScale

These advertising partners may collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers, advertising ID, and ad interaction data, in order to provide relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Rewarded Advertisements

The app may offer optional rewarded advertisements. Users may choose to watch ads voluntarily in exchange for in-app rewards such as additional coins or game benefits.

Watching ads is completely optional and not required to continue basic gameplay.

6. Data Storage

The app may store limited data related to gameplay and rewards, such as:

  • Coin balance
  • Game progress
  • Reward history

This information is used only for app functionality and improving the user experience.

7. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion from within the application if the feature is available.

Once an account is deleted, associated data such as coins and reward history may be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

8. Children's Privacy

mBrain Quiz is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
